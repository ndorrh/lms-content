# Week 1: The Foundation of Modern Web Development

## Overview
Welcome to your first week at TechAscend! This week is all about building a solid foundation. You will set up your professional development environment, master the structural language of the web (HTML), and begin styling your creations with CSS.

## Learning Objectives
- Set up VS Code, Git, and Node.js.
- Understand the difference between semantic and non-semantic HTML.
- Learn the basics of CSS selectors and the box model.
- Master the Git workflow: Init, Add, Commit, and Push.

## The Endgame: Personal Portfolio
Throughout this week, you will work on your professional portfolio. By the end of the week, you will have a live, styled webpage that tells your story to the world.
